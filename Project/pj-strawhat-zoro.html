RORONOA ZORO

- D.O.B: 11th of November
- Height: 1m81
- Strength: SWORDSMAN : Zoro enjoys fighting a strong opponent. He knows that he still has a lot of untapped potentials, and is constantly seen training and trying to improve his fighting skills. He adheres to a strict sword master's code of honor, and never falsely brags or lies about his abilities. He also never tries to escape from a fight or use trickery to win, believing that doing so is cowardly and scorning anyone who uses such tactics.
- Personality:
+ Zoro has a stern, serious, distanced personality but his will is very vigorous, and determined. + On the ship, he normally either trains with weights or sleep. He also likes sake, but he never gets drunk due to his inhumanly-high endurance and tolerance for alcohol.
+ Another notable trait is his lack of orientation, as Zoro often gets lost, even in small, familiar spaces or when being led by someone.
+ He is often the first to sense an enemy or danger and the first to react.
+ Zoro often also reminds others of the harsh facts, which often shocks some of the other crew members, although they usually decide he is right.
